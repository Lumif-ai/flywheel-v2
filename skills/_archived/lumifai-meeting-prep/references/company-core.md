# Lumif.ai — Core Brief

## What We Do
Lumif.ai is an AI-native platform that parses any contract, extracts structured compliance requirements (insurance, regulatory, SLA, indemnification, credentialing), reconciles those requirements against evidence documents (COIs, endorsements, bonds, BAAs, licenses), and surfaces gaps with full traceability. We are the reconciliation layer between contracts and evidence.

## Platform Modules
1. **Insurance Compliance** — Parse MSAs, extract insurance requirements, ingest sub documents (COI, endorsements, dec pages), generate compliance matrix with dual traceability, auto-send deficiency notices
2. **Wrap Program Management** — Parse OCIP/CCIP wrap manuals, identify wrap-provided vs. off-wrap coverages, flag excluded trades, monitor for coverage drift
3. **Vendor Pre-Qualification** — Ingest AIA A305 packages, extract financial & safety risk indicators (EMR, TRIR/DART, loss runs), validate against public sources (FDIC, OSHA, SOS), decision-ready dashboard
4. **Mid-Project Risk Monitoring** (planned, P0) — Continuous sub health monitoring post-pre-qual: OSHA enforcement, insurance status, financial signals, project performance

## Key Differentiator
Contract-to-evidence reconciliation. COI trackers check documents against manually configured rules. CLM tools store contracts. Pre-qual platforms score vendor risk. Nobody starts from actual contract language, auto-generates the requirement set, and reconciles across to the evidence documents. We do.

Sharpest technical edge: **endorsement-level parsing**. An MSA requires CG 20 10 (Additional Insured). The COI shows "Additional Insured" checked, but the actual endorsement page shows CG 20 33 (narrower coverage). No competitor parses endorsement pages for specific form numbers. We do.
