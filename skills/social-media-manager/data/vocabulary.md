# Ownable Vocabulary

## The Admin Tax
- **Definition:** The aggregate cost of useful-but-wrong-person work
- **Pillar:** The Admin Tax
- **Coined in:** "The 60% Number" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "The Automation Archipelago", "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined

## The 60% Trap
- **Definition:** Spending most time on work that feels productive but isn't your job
- **Pillar:** The Admin Tax
- **Coined in:** "The 60% Number" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined

## Productive Theater
- **Definition:** Admin that looks like real work but is re-learning what you already knew
- **Pillar:** The Admin Tax
- **Coined in:** "The 60% Number" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined

## The Automation Archipelago
- **Definition:** Many automations, no bridges between them. Each an island, you're the only ferry.
- **Pillar:** The Context Ceiling
- **Coined in:** "The Automation Archipelago" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined

## The Context Ceiling
- **Definition:** The point where isolated automations stop scaling because they can't share knowledge
- **Pillar:** The Context Ceiling
- **Coined in:** "The Automation Archipelago" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "Automating Tasks vs. Automating Knowledge", "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined

## Compound Knowledge
- **Definition:** Knowledge that grows as a side effect of doing work, not as a separate initiative
- **Pillar:** Compound Knowledge
- **Coined in:** "Automating Tasks vs. Automating Knowledge" (The Automation Architect series)
- **Referenced in:** "The Team Tax"
- **Status:** coined
