📖 Origin Story

Father Husni traveled to Miami to study, where he met Khadejah Dowd — an 18-year-old American orphan who had recently converted to Islam — outside a mosque. They married and moved to Kuwait. The family fled Kuwait on the brink of the Gulf War when Ebraheem was two, relocating to Florida.

When his parents separated (age 13), Ebraheem was the only son who chose to leave the family's luxury villa to live with his mother in a modest one-bedroom apartment. He became the sole provider for his mother at 13 — the driving motivation behind all his early entrepreneurship.

"I believe if I have the blessings of mothers, then I will continue to always be successful."
💍 Marriage History

1st (age 17): Forced by conservative father after being caught holding hands at a mall. Married in Morocco, separated shortly after.
2nd (~2016): Limited details disclosed.
3rd (March 2023): Married Hamda Al Hamly in a lavish AED 2M (~$545K) wedding at Rixos Premium Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi. Featured in Dubai Bling S2. Divorced in 2024 — became a major S3 storyline.
