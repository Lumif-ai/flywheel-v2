### Origin Story
His father Husni traveled to Miami to study, where he met Khadejah Dowd — an 18-year-old American orphan, divorcee, and mother of two who had recently converted to Islam — outside a mosque. They married and moved to Kuwait, where Ebraheem was born. The family **fled Kuwait when Ebraheem was two, on the brink of the Gulf War**, and relocated to Florida (near Disney World).

When parents separated (Ebraheem was 13), he was the only son who chose to leave the family's luxury villa to live with his mother in a modest one-bedroom apartment in Florida. Took on the role of sole provider for his mother at 13 — this became the driving motivation behind his early entrepreneurship.

Key quote: *"I believe if I have the blessings of mothers, then I will continue to always be successful."*

### Marriage Backstory
1. **First marriage (age 17):** Forced by his conservative father after being caught holding hands with a girl at a mall. Father said "if I loved her and wanted her I had to marry her." Married in Morocco, separated shortly after returning to the US.
2. **Second marriage (~2016):** Limited details disclosed.
3. **Third marriage (March 2023):** Married **Hamda Al Hamly** at Rixos Premium Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi in a lavish **AED 2 million (~$545K) wedding**. Featured prominently in Dubai Bling S2. His wife maintained privacy and did not reveal her face on camera. Divorced in 2024 — became a major storyline in Dubai Bling S3.
